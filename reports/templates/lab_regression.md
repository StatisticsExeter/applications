## The Problem

Data on Energy Certificates were downloaded in March 2025. We aim to determine whether some local authorities are better than others in reducing carbon footprints, adjusting for housing age and size.

We fit a linear mixed model:

$$Y_{ji} = \beta_0 + \beta_1 x_{j}^{(\text{old})} + \sum_{r=1}^{10} \beta_r x_{j}^{(r)} + \gamma_j$$
